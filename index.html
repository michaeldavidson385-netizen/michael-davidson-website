About me

A bit about me

Michael Davidson is an accredited investor, real estate developer, entrepreneur, and author of The Waking: How to Live the Life of Your Dreams.

After personal development helped him fundamentally reshape his own life, Michael became deeply committed to mastering what he calls the 7 areas of life — mental, physical, familial, social, vocational, financial, and spiritual — and to helping others do the same. He backs that philosophy with action, having completed Spartan Races, a full-distance Ironman, a 100-mile cross-country ski expedition, and a grueling 28-hour adventure race without sleep.

He brings 16 years of sales experience to everything he does, including a decade in enterprise technology with companies like Adobe, Quantum Metric, and Qualtrics. Michael holds a Bachelor of Business Administration from Trent University (2013) and an Executive MBA from the University of Ottawa, where he specialised in consulting.

Michael is the host of The Michael Davidson Podcast — unfiltered conversations with people achieving remarkable success across one or more areas of life. He is based in Austin, TX, and spends his summers in Ottawa, Canada.
